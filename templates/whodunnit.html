Whodunnit

Out by Ten Islands the fog sits low, violet on the water. Footprints—too small for a boot, too wide for a deer—lead from the dock to the boathouse and back again, skipping boards like a rhythm. Sheriff says wind. Yeti says rhythm. Timmy says follow the beat. The bubbles drift north; the creek runs south; somewhere between them the truth keeps time…
