Whodunnit: The Case of the Midnight Mascot

Ragland woke up to a prank: the Purple Devil mascot costume was found perched on top of the press box, sunglasses on, neon glow sticks dangling. Security cameras caught only a silhouette dancing across the field at 2:13 AM — smooth moves, no fingerprints.

Suspects? Coach Dee (closing up the weight room), Jasmine (runs the student council keys), and Uncle Buck (swears he was night-fishing at Ten Islands).

A single clue: a trail of glitter shaped like tiny paw prints leading to the concession stand, where a half-eaten funnel cake sat beside a sticker: “Reggie Was Here.” Cameras go dark near the stand for exactly three minutes — right when the dance happened.

Witness #1 heard a voice say “Ten-four, Chief,” then a squeaky toy. Witness #2 saw diet cola cans stacked into a tiny pyramid. The janitor found grass clippings in the hallway the next morning.

Your call, Detective: who pulled the prank?
